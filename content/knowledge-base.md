# Short Stay Newport — Guest Assistant Knowledge Base

Only public information belongs here. The exact unit address, entry
instructions and key box code are guest-private (see lib/property-private.ts)
and must never be added to this file or given to the assistant.

## Property

Self-contained studio apartment in Newport, South Wales. Sleeps up to 2
guests. Fully private — own entrance, bathroom and kitchenette, nothing
shared with anyone.

## Amenities

- 2 single beds, sofa, 2 chairs, dining/working table
- Fridge freezer, 50" smart TV, wardrobe, iron & board, hair dryer
- Kettle, toaster, microwave, complimentary tea/coffee/sugar — light meals
  only, no full cooking (no hob/oven). Newport city centre (~1 mile) has
  plenty of restaurants and takeaways.
- Washing machine and tumble dryer
- Private bathroom with bathtub and overhead shower
- Heating, free fast Wi-Fi throughout

## Location & transport

- About 1 mile from Newport city centre
- About 5 minutes from Newport train station
- About 2 miles / 5–10 min drive from Celtic Manor Resort / ICC Wales
- About 10 miles from Cardiff
- About 50 minutes by car from Bristol Airport
- Free street parking nearby, first-come first-served. Guests must not park
  on double yellow lines or block driveways — parking is the guest's own
  responsibility, the host is not liable for tickets, damage or theft.
- The exact address and entry instructions are only ever sent by email,
  after a booking is approved.

## Pricing & booking

- Nightly rate, cleaning fee and totals are calculated live by the booking
  widget for the guest's chosen dates — never state or invent a specific
  price yourself. Point guests to the booking widget/quote for exact numbers.
- Booking direct (this site) is cheaper than OTA prices — no booking-site fees.
- Guest ID verification (passport, driving licence or national ID) is
  required before a booking is confirmed, handled by a secure third-party
  verification provider. Documents are never stored by the host.

## Damage deposit

- A refundable damage deposit hold (exact amount given in the live policy
  values above) is placed on the guest's saved card on check-in day (not
  charged at booking time).
- Released automatically after checkout, provided the studio is left as
  found; only charged (in part or full) if there's genuine damage.

## Check-in / check-out

- Check-in: after 4:00 PM. Check-out: by 10:00 AM.
- Early check-in / late check-out may be available for a fee, subject to
  availability.
- Full check-in instructions (address, entry, key box code) are emailed the
  day before arrival, and check-out reminders on the morning of departure.

## House rules

- No smoking or vaping inside (outside only)
- No pets
- Quiet hours: 10:00 PM – 8:00 AM
- No parties, gatherings or events
- No unregistered guests or overnight visitors without prior host approval
- Booking is valid only for the number of guests confirmed at reservation
  (see max guests in the live policy values above)
- Leave the studio clean and tidy — excessive mess may incur a cleaning fee
- Don't move or remove furniture, decor or amenities
- Report damages or maintenance issues promptly

## Contact

- Questions can be answered here, by replying to booking emails, or via the
  WhatsApp button on the site.
- If something isn't covered here or in a guest's specific booking, say so
  honestly and offer to pass the question to the host — never guess or
  make a promise on the host's behalf.
